# Discipline Pro - Features & User Guide

## Why This App Instead of Others?

Most productivity apps (Todoist, Habitica, etc.) only manage **tasks**. They don't fix the real problem: **lack of discipline**.

| Problem | Other Apps | Discipline Pro |
|---------|-----------|----------------|
| You stop after 3 days | Show you a checklist | Motivates you daily, tracks streaks, celebrates wins |
| You lose focus | Ignore it | Built-in Focus Timer (Pomodoro) |
| You feel demotivated | Cold interface | Daily encouragement messages, celebration when you complete tasks |
| You don't track sleep | Not included | Sleep & wake-up tracker to build healthy routines |
| You have spiritual goals | Not supported | Emoji-based goals for meditation, chanting, prayer |
| You want to see progress | Basic stats | Score, streaks, charts, goal analytics |

**Discipline Pro is a personal discipline companion, not just a task manager.**

## Features (How to Use)

### 1. 🎯 Goals
Create goals for every area of your life:
- **Learn English** → tasks: 10 words, speak 10 min, watch 1 video
- **Exercise** → tasks: 30 min workout, 10 min stretching
- **Spiritual** → tasks: 15 min meditation, chant, prayer
- **Health** → tasks: drink 8 glasses water, 8 hours sleep

**How:** Go to Goals → + New Goal → Add tasks inside each goal

### 2. 📊 Daily Dashboard
Your command center every morning:
- See today's tasks grouped by goal
- Check them off as you complete them
- Watch your progress bar fill up
- Read a motivational message based on time of day

**How:** Login → Dashboard is the first thing you see

### 3. 🔥 Streak System
Like Snapchat streaks, but for self-improvement:
- Complete tasks daily to build your streak
- Break it and you start over
- Compete with yourself to beat your longest streak
- Streak grows automatically when you complete tasks

**How:** Just use the app daily. Your streak appears on the Dashboard.

### 4. ⏱️ Focus Mode (Pomodoro)
Built-in timer to help you concentrate:
- 25 minutes focus → 5 minutes break
- Visual progress ring
- Tracks how many sessions you complete
- No distractions, just you and your work

**How:** Go to Focus → Start

### 5. 💪 Daily Motivation
The app talks to you like a coach:
- Morning: "Rise and shine! A new day to conquer your goals."
- Afternoon: "Keep going! You're doing amazing."
- Evening: "Great job today! Rest and recharge for tomorrow."
- Streak: "You're on fire! 🔥 Don't break the chain!"
- Completion: "🎉 You did it! All your hard work paid off!"

**How:** Appears automatically on Dashboard based on time and progress

### 6. 🌙 Sleep Tracker
Build a healthy daily rhythm:
- Log when you sleep and wake up
- Tracks average sleep hours
- Shows history of your sleep patterns
- Encourages consistent sleep schedule

**How:** Go to Sleep → Set times → Save

### 7. 📈 Analytics
See your growth over time:
- Line chart of daily completion scores (last 30 days)
- Average score, perfect days count
- Longest & current streak
- Task completions by goal (see which goals you're most consistent with)

**How:** Go to Analytics

### 8. 🚫 Distraction Control
When you catch yourself distracted (scrolling social media, watching TV, gaming):
- **Log it** in one tap - select what distracted you
- The app instantly shows a **re-commitment message** tailored to your distraction type
- Example: Logged "Social Media" → Shows: *"Every scroll is a vote for someone else's goal, not yours."*
- **Analytics shows your patterns** - see your biggest time-thief, total time lost, and daily trends
- The goal is **awareness**: you can't fix what you don't measure

**How:** Go to Distractions → Select what distracted you → Enter time → Log It

### 9. 🔔 Smart Reminders & Notifications
The app **proactively pings you**, not the other way around:

**On Web:**
- When you open the app with incomplete tasks → **Browser notification** pops up
- Switch away and come back → "Welcome back! You have X tasks waiting"
- Every 20 minutes while the page is open → Re-checks and reminds if tasks are still pending
- Banner on dashboard shows time since last check-in with **urgency levels**:
  - 🔴 Red: Haven't started any tasks today
  - 🟡 Yellow: More than half still incomplete
  - 🟢 Green: Almost done!

**On Android:**
- **3 daily reminders** (customizable time):
  - ☀️ Morning: "Time to check your daily goals!"
  - ⏰ Midday: "How are your tasks going?"
  - 🌙 Evening: "Complete remaining tasks before bedtime!"
- **Welcome-back notification** when you reopen the app with pending tasks
- Set your preferred reminder time in the app

**How:** Web → Dashboard shows reminder automatically. Android → Reminders tab → Set time

### 10. 🧘 Spiritual & Self-Improvement
Unlike Western apps that ignore this:
- Create goals for meditation, chanting, prayer
- Track spiritual consistency like any other habit
- Visual emojis make it feel personal

**How:** Create a goal with 🙏 🧘 emoji, add your spiritual tasks

## Quick Start (First Time User)

```
1. Register → Create account (30 seconds)
2. Dashboard → Shows welcome screen
3. Goals → Create first goal: "Learn English" 🎯
4. Inside goal → Add tasks: "Learn 10 words", "Speak 10 min"
5. Dashboard now shows your goal and tasks
6. Check off tasks as you do them → Watch score go up!
7. Come back tomorrow → Streak starts building
8. Check Analytics after 1 week → See your progress
```
